Welcome to TestMaker

TestMaker is a solution for making individual based multichoice type assessment.

The individual assigned a test can just login and take the test.

Record Performances

TestMaker helps manage records of the performance of the individual student

Make Test

TestMaker helps make individual based multichoice test question

Take Test

TestMaker administers the examination to the assigned students.

About

TestMaker was inspired from the need to save the time cost of sorting, marking and recording examination after administering it. Allowing for easy monitoring of students progress.

As a teacher looking trying transistion to tech, my interest is to build solutions for academics challenges.
